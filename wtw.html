Where's There Word

Built in 2013 in a 2 person team "Where's the word", with its super creative name, is a simple and fun word search game intended to help pass the time. It was built for the iOS platform and supports multiplayer capabilities. Select you game mode, then choose a category and you are off to the races in finding all the words in the grid. the more words you find the better your final score so try to find them all but remember you need to HURRY before the stop clock runs out!

Take away

This was by far one of the most rewarding projects I have worked on. What started out as a mandatory project for my senior year ended up being a body of work that I was fully commited to and very passionate about. It was my first development experience where I had full autonomy over how far I wanted to take the product. I learned so much about myself as a developer and as a team member.
